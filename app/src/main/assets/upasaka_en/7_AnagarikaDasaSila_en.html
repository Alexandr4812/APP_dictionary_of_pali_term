ANAGARIKA DASA SĪLA
Ten rules of moral training for laypersons anagarika

(Lay):

OKĀSA AHAṀ BHANTE TISARANENA SADDHIṀ ANAGARIKA DASA SĪLAṂ
DHAMMAṀ YĀCĀMI, ANUGGAHAṀ KATVĀ SĪLAṀ DETHĀ ME BHANTE.
 Please, venerable one, I request the ten rules of moral training anagarika. Please be gracious and give me the precepts, venerable one.

DUTIYAMPI
TATIYAMPI

(Monk):
YAMAHAṀ VADĀMI TAṀ VADETHA.
Repeat what I say.

(Lay Devotee):
ĀMA, BHANTE.
Yes, venerable sir.

NAMO TASSA BHAGAVATO ARAHATO SAMMĀ-SAMBUDDHASSA (X3) Homage to the Blessed One, the Worthy One, the Fully Enlightened One (3 times)

BUDDHAṀ SARAṆAṀ GACCHĀMI
I go to the Buddha as my refuge

DHAMMAṀ SARAṆAṀ GACCHĀMI
I go to the Dhamma as my refuge

SAṄGHAṀ SARAṆAṀ GACCHĀMI
I go to the Sangha as my refuge

(X3)

(Monk):
TISARAṆA GAMANAṀ PARIPUṆṆAṀ.
The going to the Three Refuges is complete.

(Lay Devotee):
ĀMA BHANTE.
Yes, venerable sir.

1.
PĀNĀTIPĀTA VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
1. I undertake the training rule to abstain from killing living beings

2.
ADINNĀDĀNĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
2. I undertake the training rule to abstain from taking what is not given

3.
ABRAHMACARIYĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
3. I accept the rule of abstaining from unchaste sexual behavior.

4.
MUSĀVĀDA VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
4. I undertake the training rule to abstain from false speech

5.
SURĀMERAYA MAJJAPAMĀDAṬṬĀNĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
I undertake the training rule to abstain from consuming [alcoholic drinks:] sura, meraya and other substances that cause intoxication and heedlessness

6.
VIKALABHOJANĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
I undertake the training rule to abstain from eating at improper times [after midday].

7.
NACCA-GĪTA-VĀDITA-VISŪKADASSANĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
I accept the rule of abstaining from dancing, singing, music, and attending entertainment events.

8.
MĀLĀ-GANDHA-VILEPANA-DHĀRAṆA-MAṆḌANA-VIBHŪSANAṬṬHĀNĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
I accept the rule of abstaining from wearing garlands, using perfumes, and cosmetics for decorating the body.

9.
UCCĀSAYANĀ-MAHĀSAYANĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
I undertake the training rule to abstain from using high and luxurious beds.

10.
JĀTA RŪPA RAJATA PAṬIGGAHAṆĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
I accept the rule of abstaining from accepting gold and silver

 

(Monk):

TISARAṆENA SADDHIṂ ANAGARIKA DASA SĪLAṂ DHAMMAṂ SĀDHUKAM SURAKKHITAṂ KATVĀ APPAMĀDENA SAMPĀDETHA.
While well protecting the ten rules of moral training anagarika with the Three Refuges, practice diligently with mindfulness.

(Lay Devotee):
ĀMA, BHANTE.
Yes, venerable sir.

 

(Monk):
SĪLENA SUGATIṀ YANTI
Through morality one goes to good destinations

SĪLENA BHOGASAMPADĀ
Through morality [one attains] wealth

SĪLENA NIBBUTIṀ YANTI
Through morality one goes to liberation

TASMĀ SĪLAṀ VISODHAYE.
Therefore one should purify morality.
