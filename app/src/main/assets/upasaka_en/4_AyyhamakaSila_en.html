Gahaṭṭha Ājīva aṭṭhamaka Sīla
The Eight Precepts of Right Livelihood for Laypeople

 

(Lay Devotee):
OKĀSA AHAṀ BHANTE TISARANENA SADDHIṀ ĀJĪVA AṬṬHAMAKA SĪLAṂ DHAMMAṀ YĀCĀMI, ANUGGAHAṀ KATVĀ SĪLAṀ DETHĀ ME BHANTE.
Venerable sir, I request the Eight Precepts of Right Livelihood together with the Three Refuges. Please be compassionate and bestow these precepts upon me, venerable sir.

 

DUTIYAMPI
TATIYAMPI
(For the second time, for the third time)

(Monk):
YAMAHAṀ VADĀMI TAṀ VADETHA.
Repeat what I say.

(Lay Devotee):
ĀMA, BHANTE.
Yes, venerable sir.

NAMO TASSA BHAGAVATO ARAHATO SAMMĀ-SAMBUDDHASSA (X3) Homage to the Blessed One, the Worthy One, the Fully Enlightened One (3 times)

BUDDHAṀ SARAṆAṀ GACCHĀMI
I go to the Buddha as my refuge

DHAMMAṀ SARAṆAṀ GACCHĀMI
I go to the Dhamma as my refuge

SAṄGHAṀ SARAṆAṀ GACCHĀMI
I go to the Sangha as my refuge

(X3)

(Monk):
TISARAṆA GAMANAṀ PARIPUṆṆAṀ.
The going to the Three Refuges is complete.

(Lay Devotee):
ĀMA BHANTE.
Yes, venerable sir.

1.
PĀNĀTIPĀTA VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
1. I undertake the training rule to abstain from killing living beings

2.
ADINNĀDĀNĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
2. I undertake the training rule to abstain from taking what is not given

3.
ABRAHMACARIYĀ MICCHĀCĀRĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
3. I undertake the training rule to abstain from unchastity [and] sexual misconduct

4.
MUSĀVĀDA VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADHAṀ SAMĀDIYĀMI
4. I undertake the training rule to abstain from false speech

5.
5. PISUNĀVĀCĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṂ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
5. I undertake the training rule to refrain from slanderous speech.

6.
PHARUSĀVĀCĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṂ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
6. I undertake the training rule to refrain from harsh speech.

7.
SAMPHAPPALĀPĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṂ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
7. I undertake the training rule to refrain from idle chatter.

8.
MICCHĀ ĀJĪVĀ CA SURĀMERAYAMAJJAPAMĀDAṬṬHĀNĀ VERAMAṆĪ SIKKHĀPADAṂ SAMĀDIYĀMI.
8. I undertake the training rule to refrain from wrong livelihood and from intoxicating drinks and drugs which lead to heedlessness.

(Monk):
TISARAṆENA SADDHIṂ ĀJĪVA AṬṬHAMAKA SĪLAṂ DHAMMAṂ SĀDHUKAM SURAKKHITAṂ KATVĀ APPAMĀDENA SAMPĀDETHA.
Having well protected the Eight Precepts of Right Livelihood together with the Three Refuges, strive diligently with mindfulness.

 

 

(Lay Devotee):
ĀMA, BHANTE.
Yes, venerable sir.

 

(Monk):
SĪLENA SUGATIṀ YANTI
 Through morality one goes to good destinations

SĪLENA BHOGASAMPADĀ
Through morality [one attains] wealth

SĪLENA NIBBUTIṀ YANTI
Through morality one goes to liberation

TASMĀ SĪLAṀ VISODHAYE.
Therefore one should purify morality.
