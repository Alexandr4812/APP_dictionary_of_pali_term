Supramundane (8 or 40)
Path Cittas — Magga (4 or 20)
82
Path of Stream-Entry 1/5
Sotāpatti-magga: eradicates the first three fetters — personality view (sakkāyadiṭṭhi), doubt (vicikicchā), and clinging to rites and rituals (sīlabbataparāmāsa).
In the system of 40 cittas, one citta per each of the 5 jhānas.
83
Path of Once-Returning 1/5
Sakadāgāmi-magga: weakens sensual desire and ill-will, but does not eradicate them completely.
Such a being will return to the sensual realm only one more time.
84
Path of Non-Returning 1/5
Anāgāmi-magga: fully eradicates sensual desire and ill-will.
Such a being will never return to the sensual realm.
85
Path of Arahantship 1/5
Arahatta-magga: eradicates the remaining five fetters — desire for fine-material existence, desire for immaterial existence, conceit, restlessness, and ignorance.
Fruition Cittas — Phala (4 or 20)
86
Fruition of Stream-Entry 1/5
Sotāpatti-phala: immediately follows the magga-citta; experience of the fruit of stream-entry with Nibbāna as object.
87
Fruition of Once-Returning 1/5
Sakadāgāmi-phala: experience of the fruit of the weakening of sensual desire and ill-will.
88
Fruition of Non-Returning 1/5
Anāgāmi-phala: experience of the fruit of the full eradication of sensual desire and ill-will.
89
Fruition of Arahantship 1/5
Arahatta-phala: experience of Nibbāna with complete liberation from all fetters. Nibbāna as object.
