MAHA JAYAMANGALA GATHA
(The Great Hymn of the Victory Blessing)

 

Mahākāruṇiko nātho
The Great Compassionate Lord

 

Hitāya sabbapāṇinaṃ
For the benefit of all living beings

 

Pūretvā pāramī sabbā
Having perfected all perfections

 

Patto sambōdhimuttamaṃ
Attained the supreme enlightenment

 

Ātēna sacca vajjēna
Through this truth of speech

 

Hōtu tē jayamangalam
May you have victory and blessing

 

Jayanto bōdhiyā mūle
He who conquered at the base of the Bodhi tree

 

Sakyānaṃ nandi vaḍḍhano
Enhancing the joy of the Sakyas

 

Evaṃ mayhaṃ jayo hōtu
Thus, may victory be mine

 

Jayassu jaya maṅgalaṃ
Victory blessing, let it be

 

Sakkatvā Buddha ratanaṃ
Honoring the Buddha as a jewel

 

Ōsadhaṃ uttamaṃ varaṃ
The highest and best medicine

 

Hitaṃ dēva manussānaṃ
Beneficial for gods and humans

 

Buddha tējēna sotthinā
By the power of the Buddha

 

Nassantu paddavā sabbe
May all misfortunes disappear

 

Dukkhā vūpasamentu te
May all suffering cease

 

Sakkatvā Dhamma ratanaṃ
Honoring the Dhamma as a jewel

 

Ōsadhaṃ uttamaṃ varaṃ
The highest and best medicine

 

Pariḷāhūpasamanaṃ
Soothing to suffering

 

Dhamma tējēna sotthinā
By the power of the Dhamma

 

Nassantu paddavā sabbe
May all misfortunes disappear

 

Bhayā vūpasamentu te
May all dangers cease

 

Sakkatvā Saṅgha ratanaṃ
Honoring the Sangha as a jewel

 

Ōsadhaṃ uttamaṃ varaṃ
The highest and best medicine

 

Āhuneyyaṃ pāhuneyyaṃ
Worthy of offerings and reverence

 

Saṅgha tējēna sotthinā
By the power of the Sangha

 

Nassantu paddavā sabbe
May all misfortunes disappear

 

Rōgā vūpasamentu te
May all diseases cease

 

Yaṅkiñci ratanaṃ lōke
Whatever jewel exists in the world

 

Vijjati vividhā puthū
Varied and numerous

 

Ratanaṃ Buddha samaṃ natthi
There is none equal to the Buddha

 

Tasmā sotthi bhavantu te
Therefore, may you have well-being

 

Yaṅkiñci ratanaṃ lōke
Whatever jewel exists in the world

 

Vijjati vividhā puthū
Varied and numerous

 

Ratanaṃ Dhamma samaṃ natthi
There is none equal to the Dhamma

 

Tasmā sotthi bhavantu te
Therefore, may you have well-being

 

Yaṅkiñci ratanaṃ lōke
Whatever jewel exists in the world

 

Vijjati vividhā puthū
Varied and numerous

 

Ratanaṃ Saṅgha samaṃ natthi
There is none equal to the Sangha

 

Tasmā sotthi bhavantu te
Therefore, may you have well-being

 

Natthi me saraṇaṃ aññaṃ
There is no other refuge for me

 

Buddho me saraṇaṃ varaṃ
The Buddha is my highest refuge

 

Ētēna saccavajjēna
Through this truth of speech

 

Hōtu te jaya maṅgalaṃ
May you have victory and blessing

 

Natthi me saraṇaṃ aññaṃ
There is no other refuge for me

 

Dhammo me saraṇaṃ varaṃ
The Dhamma is my highest refuge

 

Ētēna saccavajjēna
Through this truth of speech

 

Hōtu te jaya maṅgalaṃ
May you have victory and blessing

 

Natthi me saraṇaṃ aññaṃ
There is no other refuge for me

 

Saṅgho me saraṇaṃ varaṃ
The Sangha is my highest refuge

 

Ētēna saccavajjēna
Through this truth of speech

 

Hōtu tē jaya maṅgalaṃ
May you have victory and blessing

 

Etena sacca vajjena, sotti te hotu sabbada
Through this truth of speech, may you have well-being always

 

Etena sacca vajjena, Sabba rogo vinassatu
Through this truth of speech, may all diseases be eliminated

 

Etena sacca vajjena, hotu te jaya maṅgalam
Through this truth of speech, may you have victory and blessing
