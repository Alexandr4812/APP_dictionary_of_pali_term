Suspension of monasticism

1. Rule of teaching about ejaculation

Venerables, these thirteen rules imposing suspension are for recitation.

Original story

First sub-story

Once the Buddha stayed in Savatthi in the Jeta grove, in the Anathapindika monastery. At that time, Seiyasaka was dissatisfied with spiritual life, and he became emaciated, haggard, and pale, with veins protruding from all his limbs. Venerable Udayi saw him in this state and said to him, “Seiyasaka, you look bad. Perhaps you are dissatisfied with your spiritual life?

"This is true."

“Okay, then eat as much as you want, sleep as much as you want and bathe as much as you want. And when you become dissatisfied and sensual desire overwhelms you, pluck out the seed with your hand.” “But is this allowed?”

“Yes, I do that too.”

Then Seiyasaka ate, slept and bathed as much as he wanted, and when he became dissatisfied and sensual desire overwhelmed him, he emitted semen with his hand. After some time, Seiyasaka became handsome, with round features, his face became of good color, his skin became clear. The monks who were his friends said to him, “Seiyasaka, you were thin, haggard, and pale, your veins protruded all over your limbs. But now you are beautiful, your features are round, your face is of good color, your skin is clear. Have you taken your medicine?

“No, I didn’t take it. I simply ate, slept, and bathed as much as I wanted, and when I became unsatisfied and sensual desire overwhelmed me, I ejaculated with my hand.” “Do you eat food from the offerings of believers with the same hand with which you emit semen?”

"Yes."

Monks with little desires... complained and criticized him, "How can Venerable Seiyasaka emit semen with his hand?"

They reproached Seiyasaka in various ways and reported to the teacher. The Master then assembled the order of monks and asked Seiyasaka, “Is it true Seiyasaka that you emit semen with your hand?”

"Yes teacher."

The Buddha rebuked him, “Useless man, this is not suitable, this is not proper, this is unworthy of an ascetic, this is not allowed, this should not be done. How can you emit semen with your hand? Have I not given many teachings for the sake of dispassion, and not for the sake of passion; for the sake of freedom from mustache, and not for the sake of bonds; for the sake of non-clinging, and not for the sake of clinging? When I have taught in this way, how can you be inclined towards passion, bonds and clinging? Haven't I given many teachings for the withering away of sensual desire, for purifying intoxication, for removing craving, for uprooting attachment, for cutting off the cycle of birth and death, for ending craving, for withering away, for cessation, for extinction? Haven't I given a lot of teachings for abandoning sensual pleasures, for fully understanding the perception of sensual pleasures, removing the thirst for sensual pleasures, eliminating thoughts of sensual pleasures, calming the heat of sensual pleasures? A useless person does not increase the confidence of those without it or increase the confidence of those who have it, but it does impede the confidence of those without it, and it causes some to change their minds with confidence.” Then, after reproaching Seiyasaka in various ways for the difficulty of maintaining... The Master said, “And monks, this rule of training should be recited like this:

Preliminary rule

“Intentional release of semen is a suspension offense.”

Thus the Master established a training rule for monks.

Second sub-story

One day, the monks, eating delicious food, fell asleep without awareness and clear attentiveness, and emitted semen in their sleep. They began to repent, thinking, “Master has established a training rule that deliberate emission of semen is an offense subject to suspension. We have had emission of semen during sleep and this suggests intention. Could it be that they committed a violation that would result in suspension?” They informed the teacher. He said, “Monks, intention is assumed, but not negligence. And thus, monks, this rule of training should be recited like this:

Final Rule

“Intentional emission of semen, other than during sleep, is a suspended offense.”

Definitions

Intentionally: knowing, perceiving, intending, deciding, he violates.

Seed: There are ten types of seed: blue, yellow, red, white, buttermilk color, water color, butter color, milk color, dahi color, ghee color.

Eruption: moves from its base - that's what "eruption" means.

Except during sleep: except when it occurs during sleep.

Violation entailing suspension: only a monastic order gives suspension for a violation, sends it to the beginning, places it under veneration, and rehabilitates - not several monks, not an individual. Therefore, this is called a “suspension violation.” This is the name and designation of this violation class. Therefore, it is also called a “suspension violation.”
