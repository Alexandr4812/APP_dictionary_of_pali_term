UPOSATHA DAY FOR LAYPEOPLE

 

The Eight Precepts (Sri Lankan Tradition)

 

The laypeople recite the request:

“Sādhu! Sādhu! Sādhu!


Okāsa ahaṃ bhante ti-saraṇena saddhiṃ
aṭṭhaṅga-sīlaṃ dhammaṃ yācāmi,
anuggahaṃ katvā sīlaṃ detha me bhante.
Dutiyampi okāsa… detha me bhante.
Tatiyampi okāsa… detha me bhante.”

 

The Bhikkhu:

“Yaṃ ahaṃ vadāmi taṃ vadetha.”

 

The laypeople:

“Āma, bhante.”

 

The Bhikkhu:

“Namo tassa…” (Three times)
The laypeople repeat.

 

The Bhikkhu:
“Buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Saṅghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Dutiyampi Buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Dutiyampi Dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Dutiyampi Saṅghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Tatiyampi Buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Tatiyampi Dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.
Tatiyampi Saṅghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.”
The people repeat line by line.

 

The Bhikkhu:

 “Saraṇagamanaṃ sampuṇṇaṃ.”

 

The laypeople:

 “Āma, bhante.”

Then the Bhikkhu recites the lines, which the laypeople repeat:
“Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.
Adinnādānā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.
Abrahmacariyā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.
Musāvādā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.
Surāmeraya-majja-pamādaṭṭhānā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.
Vikāla-bhojanā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.
Nacca-gīta-vādita-visūka-dassana-mālā-gandha-vilepana-dhāraṇa-maṇḍana-vibhūsanaṭṭhānā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.
Uccā-sayana mahā-sayana veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi.”
[cf. A, IV, 248–250]

 

The Bhikkhu:

“Imaṃ aṭṭhaṅga-sīlaṃ samādiyāmi.”

 

The laypeople:

“Imaṃ aṭṭhaṅga-sīlaṃ samādiyāmi.”

(Three times)

 

The Bhikkhu:

“Ti-saraṇena saddhiṃ aṭṭhaṅga-sīlaṃ dhammaṃ sādhukaṃ surakkhitaṃ katvā appamādena sampādetha.”

 

The laypeople:

“Āma, bhante.”

 

The Bhikkhu concludes:
“Sīlena sugatiṃ yanti,
Sīlena bhoga-sampadā,
Sīlena nibbutiṃ yanti,
Tasmā sīlaṃ visodhaye.”
