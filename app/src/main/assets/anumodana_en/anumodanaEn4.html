CULLA-MAṄGALA-CAKKA-VĀḶA

 

Sabba-buddh'ānubhāvena
Sabba-dhamm'ānubhāvena
Sabba-saṅgh'ānubhāvena
By the power of all Buddhas,
by the power of all Dhamma,
by the power of all Saṅgha,

 

Buddha-ratanaṃ
Dhamma-ratanaṃ
Saṅgha-ratanaṃ
By the jewel of the Buddha,
by the jewel of the Dhamma,
by the jewel of the Saṅgha,

 

Tiṇṇaṃ ratanānaṃ ānubhāvena
Catur-āsīti-sahassa-dhammakkhandh'ānubhāvena
By the power of the three jewels,
by the power of the eighty-four thousand sections of the Dhamma,

 

Piṭakattay'ānubhāvena
Jina-sāvak'ānubhāvena
By the power of the three baskets (Tipiṭaka),
by the power of the disciples of the Conqueror,

 

Sabbe te rogā
Sabbe te bhayā
Sabbe te antarāyā
Sabbe te upaddavā
Sabbe te dunnimittā
Sabbe te avamaṅgalā vinassantu
May all your illnesses,
all your fears,
all your obstacles,
all your dangers,
all your evil omens,
all your inauspicious signs be destroyed.

 

Āyu-vaḍḍhako
Dhana-vaḍḍhako
Siri-vaḍḍhako
Yasa-vaḍḍhako
Bala-vaḍḍhako
Vaṇṇa-vaḍḍhako
Sukha-vaḍḍhako
Hotu sabbadā
May there always be an increase in longevity,
wealth,
glory,
status,
strength,
beauty,
and happiness.

 

Dukkha-roga-bhayā verā sokā sattu c'upaddavā
Anekā antarāyā pi vinassantu ca tejasā
May suffering, disease, danger, enmity, sorrow, misfortune, adversity—countless obstacles—disappear through the majesty of the Three Jewels.

 

 

Jaya-siddhi dhanaṃ lābhaṃ sotthi bhāgyaṃ sukhaṃ balaṃ
Siri āyu ca vaṇṇo ca bhogaṃ vuḍḍhī ca yasavā
Sata-vassā ca āyū ca jīva-siddhī bhavantu te
Victory, success, wealth, gain, safety, fortune, happiness, strength, glory, longevity, beauty, prosperity, and status may grow; a lifespan of one hundred years and success in your endeavors—may all this be yours.

 

Bhavatu sabba-maṅgalaṃ…
May every auspicious blessing be yours, may all the deities protect you; by the power of all Buddhas, Dhamma, and Saṅgha, may you always be well.
