This application is a reference guide for Buddhist (Theravada) monks and laypeople.

 

Our application is specifically created for monks and laypeople practicing in monasteries of the Sri Lankan Mahanikaya lineage, such as Ambokote and Chittaviveka (samatha-vipassana.com), as well as other monasteries of this lineage. It emphasizes texts and verses that are most often recited in these monasteries and which monks usually memorize. The application also contains a list of monastic rules and instructions that a monk should know and be able to apply.

 

The application is also supplemented with information from the Abhidhammatha Sangaha, which in the future should become a convenient tool for review and analysis.

 

The application also contains Suttas from the Pali Canon (taken from the website theravada.ru), the life story of the Buddha, and lectures by the abbot of the monastery - Ven. Nyanaseeha Rakwane Thero.

 

This reference guide can be used by both monks and novices in the learning process, as well as by laypeople for learning vandana texts, familiarizing themselves with the Pali Canon, the life story of the Buddha, and studying the Dhamma.

 

May those who suffer be freed from suffering;

May those who are afraid be freed from fear;

May those who grieve be freed from grief;

And may all beings be freed from suffering, fear, and grief.
